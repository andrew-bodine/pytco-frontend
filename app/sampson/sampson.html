Renovations

Phase One

This campaign will raise funds to provide street side facade renovations and basic interior rehabilitation so that the theater can open seasonally. The budget for Phase One is $340,000.

away from our first fundraising goal!

Step 1: Open the Doors

Fund new front doors to replace the current unattractive barn style door and complete a survey for hazardous materials. The cost for this Project is approximately $67,500.

These new doors will signal to the community and donors that PYTCo is making progress. The new door will allow ease of entry for building tours and will freshen up the facade.

This will create momentum for the ongoing fundraising efforts.

PYTCo hopes to install the door by the Summer of 2017.
